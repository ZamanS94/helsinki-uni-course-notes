The Seminar on Edge Intelligence (5 ECTS) is an advanced level research focused course offered in English as part of the Masterâ€™s Programme in Computer Science at the University of Helsinki. It explores how edge devices such as smartphones and IoT nodes can locally process data to reduce latency and improve efficiency especially in the context of large language models (LLMs). Students engage with cutting edge topics like distributed learning task offloading and semantic communication through presentations based on peer reviewed articles. The course includes expert guest lectures and emphasizes academic skills such as scientific writing and presentation. Assessment is based on participation the quality of the presentation and a reflective learning diary. A background in machine learning data science or IoT is recommended.
